About LocateIQ

LocateIQ is built by people passionate about data, design, and intelligent solutions. More than just a platform, we are a team dedicated to transforming how businesses choose warehouse locations and optimize supply chains. We learn, innovate, and grow together — turning complex challenges into smarter, efficient outcomes worth celebrating.
