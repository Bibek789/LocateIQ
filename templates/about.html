About LocateIQ

LocateIQ is a smart solution for optimizing warehouse placement across industries. By analyzing store and demand locations, and considering crucial factors like cost, distance, capacity, and storage type, we provide data-driven recommendations. Our tool leverages AI to help you make strategic decisions, ensuring your logistics network is efficient and cost-effective.
